Ryan Reynolds is a Canadian actor, entrepreneur, and comedian. He has starred in many well known movies such as Deadpool, Safehouse and Change-Up, and is co-owner of Aviation Gin and Mint Mobile. He is married to Blake Lively and has three children. He is well known for his role in some big-budget movies, in addition to his witty and comedic comments and character.
