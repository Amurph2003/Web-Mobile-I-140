About The Pizza Stop

Who We Are

Still in our original location on State Street right in Downtown Rochester, we were the first in the area to offer New York style thin crust, stuffed and specialty pizzas. We have been using the original recipe from our family's first pizzeria that opened in the early 1950s in Brooklyn, New York. In 1986, we opened The Pizza Stop to bring authentic New York pizza to Rochester.

The Pizza Stop not only offers authentic pizza, but our atmosphere and service put you in a New York state of mind as well. We provide a family-friendly atmosphere with great food and exceptional service at an affordable price.

From the very beginning, we have been innovative by combining new flavors to come up with creative and delicious specialty pizzas.
